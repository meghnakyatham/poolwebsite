How It Works

From First Call
to First Swim

Most pools complete in 8–12 weeks. Here's exactly what happens.

Week 1 — Free Consultation & Design

We visit your yard, take measurements, and present 3D design concepts. You approve the design and sign — no surprises.

Weeks 2–4 — Permits & Site Prep

We handle all city permits, HOA approvals, and utility locates. Most permits take 2–4 weeks in Texas.

Weeks 4–6 — Excavation & Shell

The dig begins. Your pool shell is installed — fiberglass pools are in within 1–3 days; concrete takes 2–3 weeks to cure.

Weeks 6–10 — Plumbing, Electrical & Decking

All plumbing, electrical, automation, lighting, and coping is completed. Deck is poured and cured.

Week 10–12 — Final Inspection & Handover

City inspection signed off. We fill your pool, balance the water, demonstrate all equipment, and hand you the keys.

Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know
Before Building a Pool

In Texas, a vinyl liner pool typically runs $35,000–$55,000 installed. Fiberglass pools cost $45,000–$75,000. Concrete/gunite pools start at $60,000 and can reach $120,000+ for large luxury builds. All prices include permits, excavation, equipment, and finishing. Use our cost calculator above to get an instant estimate.
Most pools are complete in 8–12 weeks from signing. Fiberglass pools are the fastest — the shell drops in within 1–3 days, and the full project wraps in 6–10 weeks. Concrete pools take longer due to the curing process, typically 10–16 weeks. Permit timelines (2–4 weeks in Texas) are usually the biggest variable.
Yes — 100%. We pull every city permit, submit all HOA documents, schedule utility locates, and coordinate all inspections. You will never have to call a county office or fill out a permit application. We've done this in every major Texas city and know exactly how to navigate each jurisdiction.
We partner with Lyon Financial and HFS Financial — two of the most competitive pool financing lenders in the country. You can get pre-approved in under 5 minutes with no hard credit pull. Terms range from 5 to 20 years, with rates starting at 6.99% for qualified borrowers. Financing starts from $299/month for a typical fiberglass pool.
Every pool we build carries a lifetime structural warranty backed by the manufacturer (fiberglass shells only). Additionally, we provide a 5-year workmanship warranty covering plumbing, electrical, decking, and coping. Equipment is covered under the manufacturer's warranty, which we register on your behalf at handover.
Fiberglass is the best choice for most homeowners — faster to install, lower lifetime maintenance costs (70% fewer chemicals), and a lifetime structural warranty. Choose concrete if you want a completely custom shape, vanishing edge, beach entry, or very large pool beyond standard fiberglass mold sizes. We'll help you make the right call during your free consultation.
In Texas, a well-built inground pool typically adds 5–8% to home value, and in high-demand neighborhoods it can reach 10–15%. The ROI is higher in Texas than most states due to the long swimming season. A fiberglass pool with low maintenance costs is particularly attractive to future buyers.
Yes — Texas law requires a barrier (fence, wall, or approved pool cover) around all residential pools. The barrier must be at least 48 inches tall with a self-closing, self-latching gate. We handle all safety barrier requirements as part of your permit package and final inspection sign-off.
